Our Story

The trails end concept was born out of a desire to bring great food and good times to an area here in El Paso that was a bit short on both.

We strive to provide a great place to grab a bite and a drink after your round of golf or after a tough day on the job.

We strive to provide a next level event experience with the staff and scenery to match.

Please let us know how your visit is and if we can improve in any way. Drink Up!
