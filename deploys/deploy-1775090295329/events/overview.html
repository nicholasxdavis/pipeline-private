Butterfield Trail Golf Club is a wedding and event venue in El Paso, Texas, located just east of Franklin Mountain State Park. This spacious clubhouse and outdoor venue overlooks our beautiful golf course, providing a unique backdrop to your wedding or special event. Conveniently located near the El Paso International Airport, this wedding venue is not far off the beaten path and provides your wedding with an atmosphere of the old west.

Facilities and Capacity

Butterfield Trail Golf Club offers multiple indoor and outdoor venues for ceremonies and receptions, accommodating up to 300 guests. The clubhouse offers an ideal setting for elegant affairs, surrounded by panoramic scenic views of the world class designed golf course. Our restaurant, The Trails End, offers a captivating views of El Paso and a rustic charm that will leave a memorable impression on your guests. The Lake View Terrace and spacious Lake View Lawn provide outdoor space for affairs of any size, allowing you and your guests to enjoy an evening under the stars.

Services Offered

Butterfield Trail Golf Club is an ideal location for wedding ceremonies and receptions, as well as rehearsal dinners, Quinceanera's, and other special family occasions. Our event coordinator and professional staff will ensure that your special event is specifically designed to exceed your expectations. We offer a variety of wedding packages with services that allow you to custom design your reception, including catering options and bar services. Other wedding services include:

  • Chairs and tables
  • Set up
  • Clean up
  • Event planning
  • China
  • Flatware
  • Glassware
  • Linens
  • Parking

Cuisine

The culinary staff at Butterfield Trail Golf Club offers several catering options in order to provide you with a customizable cuisine experience that will delight each of your guests. Their restaurant, The Trails End, offers an ideal location for receptions and rehearsal dinners that specialize in delicious breakfast and scrumptious lunch. Their catering services include china, glassware, linens, barware, and servers, as well as other catering and bar services such as:

  • Buffet
  • Cocktail reception
  • Hors d'Oeuvres
  • Plated
  • Stations
  • Bartender(s)
  • Cash bar, limited bar, or open bar
  • Champagne toast
  • Signature drink
